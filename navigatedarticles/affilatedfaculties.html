Who are we and What are we doing?

Introduction article around User-Centric Group

Xiangjun Peng Aug 17 · 5 min. read
Share this

Directed by Professor Xu Sun, User-Centric Computing Group is a sub-group of Innovative Design Lab, University of Nottingham Ningbo China.

Missions. we are User-Centric Computing Group, with the aim to introduce advanced design ideas into existing computing platforms through softwares and hardwares. We are responsible to develop practical systems to allow users to explore the state-of-the-art designs.

Current Members. User-Centric Computing Group has six undergraduate students, and four of them are working on related projects. They are Weikai Kong, Xiangjun Peng, Zili Song and Shuolei Wang (in alphabatical order).

Alumi. Previous members included Hongzhi Zhang (currently Software Engineer in Liangjing Tech) and Xiaosong Zhou (currently Assistant Professor in Guizhou Normal University).
